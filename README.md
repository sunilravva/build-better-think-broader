## About This Newsletter

Build Better, Think Broader is a product management and leadership newsletter that explores:

- 📊 **Product Strategy**: Frameworks for building winning products
- 👥 **Leadership**: Insights on building and leading high-performing teams
- 🏗️ **Enterprise Architecture**: Designing scalable platforms and systems
- 🤖 **AI & Data**: Implementing data platforms and AI solutions
- 💡 **Innovation**: Driving transformation in large organizations
- 🚀 **Career Growth**: Advancing as a product leader
